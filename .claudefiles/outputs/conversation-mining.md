5. **Regional specificity as emotional anchor** - use real place names (streets, motels, cities) to ground abstract pain in concrete reality.

6. **Accountability as rare commodity** - what happens when two people refuse to own their shit? Blame as currency, conscience-scrubbing transactions.

7. **"The lie I tell myself while I [action]"** - self-awareness during self-destruction. Promises made mid-relapse.

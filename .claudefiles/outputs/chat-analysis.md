## Reusable Insights

### Insight: Syllable matching preserves music across lyric changes
**Apply to:** Any song iteration where you want to keep the melody/flow
**Technique:** Count syllables per line, match stress patterns

### Insight: "My drug is [abstract concept]" - powerful addiction metaphor
**Apply to:** Songs about non-substance addictions (relationships, ambition, nostalgia)
**Why it works:** Subverts expected drug imagery, shows psychological dependency

### Insight: Mutual blame narrative > one-sided victim story
**Apply to:** Relationship songs seeking depth and realism
**Why it works:** Both characters flawed = more relatable, less preachy

### Insight: Regional specificity > generic locations
**Apply to:** Any narrative song wanting authenticity
**Examples:** "Sprinkle Road" and "K'zoo" ground the story in lived reality

### Insight: Production tags in Suno are metadata, not lyrics
**Apply to:** All Suno generations
**Critical:** [Mood:], [Instrument:], [Vocal Tone:] steer AI without being sung
